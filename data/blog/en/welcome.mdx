After a long time stuck with a generic placeholder, I finally took the time to rebuild this site. The idea was always the same: have a place of my own to share what I build and what I learn.

## What you'll find here

- **Build notes**: real problems I run into while developing Zendinit, Yappie and other projects, plus challenges from my 9-to-6. Concrete solutions, no fluff.
- **Side projects**: every new project I start will have its own space here, with technical context and design decisions.
- **Reflections**: every now and then I'll write about what it means to bootstrap alone, bet on your own ideas, and live between Cuba and Spain.

## The stack behind this site

In case you're curious about the technical detail: Next.js 16 with App Router, Contentlayer for MDX content, Tailwind CSS for styles, and next-intl for Spanish/English bilingual support.

Nothing groundbreaking, but it works well for what I need: write in Markdown, publish fast, and not depend on any external CMS.

## What's next

I have several drafts in the pipeline on frontend architecture, state patterns, and productivity tools for developers. If any of that interests you, come back soon.
